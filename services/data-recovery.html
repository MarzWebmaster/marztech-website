Overview

When Data Loss Strikes, We're Here to Help

Data loss can be devastating for any business or individual. At Marz Technology, we provide professional data recovery services that give you the best possible chance of retrieving your critical files, databases, and documents. Our specialists use advanced techniques, specialized tools, and controlled environments to recover data from a wide range of storage devices and failure scenarios.

We handle everything from simple accidental deletions to complex physical damage cases. Our recovery process is thorough and methodical, with a focus on data integrity and confidentiality. We never recover data to the original drive to avoid further damage, and we provide clear communication throughout the process.

What We Offer

Key Features & Capabilities

Our comprehensive service includes the following capabilities, delivered by certified professionals using industry best practices.

Hard Drive Recovery

Recovery of data from failed, damaged, or corrupted hard disk drives (HDDs) including mechanical failures, bad sectors, and firmware issues.

RAID Data Recovery

Advanced recovery from RAID 0, 1, 5, 6, 10, and proprietary RAID configurations. Handling multiple drive failures, controller failures, and rebuild issues.

SSD & Flash Recovery

Recovery from solid-state drives, USB flash drives, memory cards, and other flash-based storage devices with controller failures or logical corruption.

Deleted File Recovery

Recovery of accidentally deleted files, formatted partitions, lost volumes, and corrupted file systems using advanced scanning and reconstruction techniques.

Database & Email Recovery

Specialized recovery of corrupted database files (SQL, MySQL, Oracle) and email stores (Exchange, Outlook PST). Minimal data loss guaranteed.

Secure Data Erasure

Certified secure data destruction and erasure services for drives and storage media that need to be decommissioned or disposed of securely.
