Overview

Comprehensive Security for Your Peace of Mind

Security is a top priority for any business. At Marz Technology, we provide advanced CCTV and security systems that give you complete visibility and control over your premises. From high-definition surveillance cameras to integrated access control systems, our solutions are designed to deter threats, monitor activity, and provide critical evidence when needed.

We tailor every security system to your specific requirements, whether you're securing a small retail store, a large warehouse, an office building, or an industrial facility. Our team handles everything from site assessment and system design to installation, configuration, and remote monitoring.

What We Offer

Key Features & Capabilities

Our comprehensive service includes the following capabilities, delivered by certified professionals using industry best practices.

HD & IP Surveillance Cameras

High-definition IP cameras with night vision, motion detection, and remote viewing capabilities. Indoor, outdoor, dome, bullet, and PTZ options available.

Network Video Recorders (NVR)

Centralized recording and management of all camera feeds with configurable storage, smart search, and mobile app access for real-time monitoring.

Access Control Systems

Keycard, biometric, and PIN-based access control for doors, gates, and restricted areas. Integration with time attendance and visitor management.

Intrusion Detection & Alarms

Advanced alarm systems with motion sensors, glass break detectors, and perimeter protection. Instant alerts to your phone and central monitoring station.

Remote Monitoring & Mobile Access

View live and recorded footage from anywhere via smartphone, tablet, or desktop. Receive instant alerts for motion events and system alerts.

Security System Integration

Seamless integration of CCTV, access control, alarms, and intercom systems into a single unified platform for centralized management.
