Our Story

Building Technology, Building Trust

Founded in 2015, Marz Technology & Trading has grown from a small IT consultancy into a comprehensive technology solutions provider serving businesses across Malaysia. Based in Bandar Puncak Alam, Selangor, we have built a reputation for delivering reliable, innovative, and cost-effective IT solutions that help our clients thrive in an increasingly digital world.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to make enterprise-grade technology accessible to every Malaysian business, regardless of size. In our early days, we focused on IT support and networking solutions for small businesses in the Klang Valley area — Shah Alam, Subang Jaya, Petaling Jaya, and the surrounding communities of Selangor. Word of our dedication, technical skill, and honest approach spread quickly, and our client base grew organically through referrals and repeat business.

By 2018, we had expanded our service portfolio to include CCTV and security systems, web development, and cloud solutions. This period of growth allowed us to serve mid-sized enterprises and government-linked organisations, building a track record of successful projects across multiple industries. Our team grew from a handful of technicians to a diverse group of certified engineers, consultants, and project managers — each bringing specialised expertise to the table.

In 2021, we made a strategic investment in artificial intelligence and automation capabilities, recognising early that AI would transform how Malaysian businesses operate. This forward-thinking decision positioned us as one of the leading AI solutions providers in the Klang Valley, helping clients in Bandar Puncak Alam, Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, and beyond leverage intelligent automation to streamline operations and reduce costs.

Today, in 2026, Marz Technology & Trading stands as a full-service technology partner with over 200 satisfied clients and more than 500 completed projects. We serve businesses throughout Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, Putrajaya, and the wider Klang Valley — from our office at Pusat Perdagangan Hillpark in Bandar Puncak Alam to client sites across the nation. Our commitment to excellence, integrity, and genuine partnership remains the foundation of everything we do.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality and client satisfaction. We don't just provide technology — we become a trusted partner in your success. Every solution we deliver is backed by years of hands-on experience, deep technical expertise, and a genuine passion for helping businesses leverage technology to achieve their goals.

From small and medium enterprises to large corporations, our clients trust us to keep their IT infrastructure running smoothly, their data secure, and their teams productive. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to staying at the forefront of technology — embracing AI, cloud computing, and automation to help our clients stay ahead of the curve.
